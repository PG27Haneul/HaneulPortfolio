Unity C# 2D Game Development AI Programming

Project Overview

Neon Nexus is a Top Down, 2.5D, Stealth game. Where you play as the repair bot Roko, who has broken free from the AI Hive Mind. While Navigating the Maze Like floors of the Basilisk Tower you will use Roko’s Omni-Tool to temporarily Disable Patrol Bots and to Solve Puzzles while progressing to the super computer at the top of the tower in order to Destroy the AI Hive Mind and free the other robots.

Key Features

  • AI System: Enemies follow set patrol routes, avoid obstacles, and use vision cones to detect the player in real-time
  • Enemy Variety: Includes ground patrols, flying enemies that can see over walls, and fixed-position cameras watching key areas
  • Combat: The player can shoot ground enemies to stun them and create an opening to escape or sneak past

Technical Highlights

  • Enemies patrol predefined paths and switch to chase mode when the player is detected
  • Player detection is handled through raycast-based vision cones that check line-of-sight in real time
  • Ground enemies can be stunned by player projectiles, temporarily disabling their behavior
  • player detection meter that fills when in enemy vision

My Role

As a programmer on this project, I was responsible for:

  • Implementing the core AI behavior systems
  • Optimizing performance and memory usage for smooth gameplay
  • Developing raycast-based vision cones with in-game visual representation
  • Integrating audio and VFX feedback for enemy states and player actions

Unreal Engine C++ 3D Game Development Horror Game Design

Project Overview

Sweet Spot is a first-person puzzle-horror game where you play as a hotel concierge trapped in a looping hallway by an eldritch horror. Manage your Madness Meter—Sane, The Sweet Spot, and Mad. While in The Sweet Spot, use your Focus ability to reveal hidden items, solve puzzles, and find the missing elevator pieces to escape.

Key Features

  • Focus Ability: Unique mechanic allowing players to reveal hidden clues and environmental hints
  • Atmospheric Horror: Psychological tension built through environmental storytelling and sound design
  • Madness Meter: Sane (neutral), Sweet Spot (activates Focus to reveal hidden items), Mad (vignette and chromatic aberration); Madness increases in rooms and decreases in hallways

Technical Highlights

  • Tentacle and eye entities that fade when looked at while in Focus mode
  • Smooth camera with adjustable FOV, vignette, and chromatic aberration effects
  • Objects that fade in and out of reality based on the player’s Madness

My Role

As a programmer, I handled:

  • Focus ability implementation
  • UI popups for item pickups and environmental feedback
  • Camera effects (shake, FOV, post-processing) to enhance player interaction and immersion
  • Tentacle and eye entities that react to Focus
  • Teleporting doors used for puzzles and maze navigation
